Welcome to Charity Rush!

Charity Rush is a one-of-a-kind charity event that combines fun, adrenaline, and giving back! Our park is filled with exciting parkour buildings that offer a thrilling 2-hour experience for all ages. By purchasing a ticket, you'll not only test your skills but also contribute to a good cause.

How Can I Participate?

The event will be held on April 22nd (Sunday) at South Park, open all day starting at 11:00 AM. Come and join us for a day of fun, adventure, and making a difference.

About the Parkour

Our parkour trials are designed to be challenging yet enjoyable for all ages, with an emphasis on young adults. The park is aimed to be challenging but also fun for all ages, with an emphasis on young adults. And, with trampolines under each building and a medical team on hand, safety is our top priority.

Supporting a Good Cause

All proceeds from the event will go towards supporting the Feed The Children charity, which aims to raise money to feed children suffering from starvation and in desperate need of food. By participating in Charity Rush, you'll be making a difference in the global community and we are grateful for your support. If you're unable to attend, you can still make a difference by directly donating to the charity at https://www.feedthechildren.org/

Stay Up to Date
